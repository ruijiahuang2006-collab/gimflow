### A. Participant Characteristics

Background survey was completed by 22 respondents, of whom 21 correspond to the 23 participants included in the Study 1 analysis. One respondent did not complete the two-session protocol and is excluded from analysis; two clean-analysis participants did not submit background surveys. All 10 Study 2 participants submitted surveys. Table A1 reports the distributions over the 21 Study 1 matched respondents and the 10 Study 2 respondents. Both studies drew from a similar demographic pool of young Chinese undergraduates.
