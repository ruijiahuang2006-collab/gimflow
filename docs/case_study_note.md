# Case Study P408J7U — Extended Details

Extended details for the case study referenced in the paper (§5.5) are
provided in the paper itself, in Appendix E ("Extended Case Study Details").
Appendix E includes:

- Session identification (participant hash, session duration, condition order)
- Pre/post questionnaires (SAM, PANAS)
- Emotion planning (focus theme translated from Chinese, current and target
  VA coordinates, four-waypoint trajectory)
- Blueprint structure (four sections mapped one-to-one with waypoints)
- Dialogue excerpts from Music & Imaging and Postlude phases (translated
  from Chinese)

Individual session-level content beyond what is shown in Appendix E is
not publicly released. This is consistent with the paper's Ethics
Statement (§9), which describes the participant-privacy commitments
made at consent time. Access requests for controlled analysis purposes
may be sent to the corresponding author.
